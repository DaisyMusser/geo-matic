Bike Geometry Calculator

Changing the travel on your telescoping suspension fork will change your bicycle's geometry. Say you increase your forks travel by 10mm. This will raise your bottom bracket height, lengthen your wheelbase (very slightly) and slacken your head tube angle. But by how much?? Find out below...

The only information needed to calculate your new headtube angle and wheelbase is your stock head tube angle and wheelbase. Wheelbase the distance from center of front axel to center of rear (in mm). Head tube angle is the angle found between your steering axis and the ground (in degrees). Please enter your stock Wheelbase, stock head tube anlge, and amount of travel you are adding to your fork.
